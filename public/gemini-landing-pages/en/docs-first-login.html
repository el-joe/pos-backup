First Login & Dashboard Tour

Welcome to your workspace. This guide explains how to access your account for the first time, secure your profile, and navigate the main dashboard.

1. Accessing Your Account

After completing the registration or checkout process, check your email inbox for a welcome email from noreply@mohaaseb.com.

Tip: If you don't see the email within 5 minutes, please check your Spam/Junk folder.

  1. Click the "Verify Email" button in the email.
  2. You will be redirected to the login page.
  3. Enter the email and password you created during checkout.

2. Setting up Security (2FA)

For financial data security, we highly recommend enabling Two-Factor Authentication immediately.

  1. Click on your Avatar in the top right corner.
  2. Select Account Security.
  3. Toggle Two-Factor Authentication to ON.
  4. Scan the QR code using Google Authenticator or Authy.

3. The Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you will land on the Dashboard. This is your command center.
