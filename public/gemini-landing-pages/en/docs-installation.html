Installation & Setup Guide

Since Mohaaseb is a cloud-based solution, there is no software to install on your computer. This guide covers the initial configuration required to make your account tax-compliant and ready for operations.

Prerequisites

Ensure you have your Company Commercial Registration (CR) and VAT Number handy before proceeding.

1. Configure Company Profile

Navigate to Settings > General Settings. Accurate information here is critical for ZATCA validation.

  1. Upload your Company Logo (recommended size: 300x100px).
  2. Enter your VAT Number (15 digits starting and ending with 3).
  3. Fill in the National Address (Building No, Street, District, City, Zip Code).

2. POS Hardware Setup

Mohaaseb POS works with most USB and Network thermal printers. We recommend Epson TM-T20III or any generic printer supporting ESC/POS commands.

Connecting a USB Printer

If you are using the QZ Tray method for direct printing:

POS Config JSON 
{
  "printer_name": "EPSON_TM_T20III",
  "paper_width": "80mm",
  "auto_cut": true,
  "cash_drawer_kick": true,
  "print_logo": true
}

3. Registering ZATCA Device (CSID)

To enable Phase 2 integration, you must generate a Cryptographic Stamp Identifier (CSID).

  • Go to Settings > ZATCA Integration.
  • Click Generate CSR.
  • Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number on the Fatoora Portal.
  • Click Request Production CSID.
