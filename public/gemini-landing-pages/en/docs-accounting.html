Accounting Module

The heart of Mohaaseb ERP. Our accounting module is a fully double-entry bookkeeping system designed to automate financial tracking, ensuring accuracy and compliance with international standards (IFRS) and local regulations.

1. Chart of Accounts (COA)

Mohaaseb comes with a default Chart of Accounts tailored for your industry. You can customize this to fit your specific needs.

Structure

  • 1000-1999 Assets
  • 2000-2999 Liabilities
  • 3000-3999 Equity
  • 4000-4999 Income
  • 5000+ Expenses

To add a new account:

  1. Go to Accounting > Chart of Accounts.
  2. Click + New Account.
  3. Select the Type (e.g., Current Asset, Operating Expense).
  4. Enter a unique code and name.

2. Journal Entries

While most entries are automated via Invoices and Bills, you can create manual Journal Entries for adjustments, depreciation, or opening balances.

  • Navigate to Accounting > Journal Entries.
  • Ensure your Debits and Credits match; otherwise, the system will prevent saving.
  • Attach supporting documents directly to the entry for audit purposes.

3. Financial Reporting

Generate real-time reports to understand your financial health.

Balance Sheet

A snapshot of your Assets, Liabilities, and Equity at a specific date.

Profit & Loss (P&L)

Detailed breakdown of revenues, costs, and expenses over a period.

Trial Balance

List of all accounts and their balances to ensure books are balanced.

Tax Return Report

Auto-generated VAT report compatible with ZATCA filing.
