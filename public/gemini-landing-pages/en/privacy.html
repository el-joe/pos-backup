Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

At Mohaaseb ("we", "us", or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our cloud ERP services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or device.

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, and IP address.
  • Business Data: Company name, tax identification numbers (VAT/TIN), and financial data entered into the system.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our services, including access times, pages viewed, and the device used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service (Accounting, HR, POS).
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To provide customer support and technical assistance.
  • To process payments and generate invoices.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., ZATCA tax reporting requirements).

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement industry-standard security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, regular malware scanning, and secure cloud hosting on AWS servers. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (e.g., payment processors, email delivery services).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any incomplete or inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to the processing of your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@mohaaseb.com
  • By visiting this page on our website: Contact Page
