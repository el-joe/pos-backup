Why we started

In 2020, we noticed a gap. Legacy ERP systems were clunky, expensive, and required months to implement. Simple accounting apps were too basic for growing teams.

We built Mohaaseb to be the middle ground—powerful enough for enterprises, yet simple enough for startups. Fully integrated with ZATCA and local regulations from day one.
