What a Sales and Inventory Management System Actually Does

At its core, a sales and inventory management system tracks three things simultaneously: what you sold, what you have left, and what it's worth. Mohaaseb links these automatically — the moment a POS terminal records a sale, the ERP layer adjusts stock levels, updates the general ledger, and recalculates cost of goods sold. No manual re-entry, no reconciliation at month end.
