mohaaseb.com is a cloud platform that combines ERP, POS, accounting, and purchase and inventory management in one place. Instead of running separate tools for sales, purchasing, stock, and bookkeeping, businesses run everything through mohaaseb and keep a single source of truth.

What Is Mohaaseb.com?

Mohaaseb is built for small and mid-sized businesses that need serious accounting and inventory control without the complexity of enterprise software. It runs in the browser, works across multiple branches, and is available in both English and Arabic.

Core Modules Inside Mohaaseb

  • Point of Sale (POS): fast checkout, receipts, returns, and multi-payment support.
  • ERP & Accounting: chart of accounts, journal entries, and financial statements.
  • Purchasing: purchase requests, purchase orders, and supplier management.
  • Inventory Management: multi-warehouse stock, barcode support, and stock transfers.
  • Reporting: real-time dashboards for sales, margins, and cash flow.

How ERP, POS, Purchasing, and Inventory Connect

The strength of mohaaseb.com is that these modules are not bolted together — they share the same database. A sale on the POS updates the ERP ledger and reduces inventory instantly. A purchase order received from a supplier updates stock and cost in purchase and inventory management without any manual re-entry.

ModuleWhat It Solves

Bottom line: Mohaaseb.com is one login, one database, and one workflow for everything from a customer sale to a supplier purchase to a financial statement.

Why Businesses Choose Mohaaseb

Business owners choose mohaaseb because it removes the manual work of reconciling separate POS and ERP systems, gives accountants clean real-time books, and gives operations teams accurate inventory numbers they can trust.

Getting Started with Mohaaseb.com

New businesses can set up their chart of accounts, add products, connect a warehouse, and start selling through the POS within the same day. As the business grows, purchase and inventory management, multi-branch support, and advanced reporting scale with it — all inside mohaaseb.com.
