What Is Cloud General Accounting?

Cloud general accounting means your ledgers, journal entries, invoices, and financial statements are hosted online instead of on a single desktop machine. With mohaaseb.com, every sale recorded at the POS flows automatically into your general ledger — no manual re-entry, no end-of-day reconciliation headaches.

Why Businesses Are Moving to the Cloud

  • Access anywhere: Check your accounts from any branch, any device, any time.
  • Automatic backups: Your financial data is safe even if a local device fails.
  • Real-time sync between ERP and POS: Sales, purchases, and inventory update your books instantly.
  • Lower IT overhead: No servers to maintain — just log in to mohaaseb.com and get to work.

A trusted محاسب (accountant) still matters — cloud accounting doesn't replace expertise, it gives your accountant better, faster data to work with.
