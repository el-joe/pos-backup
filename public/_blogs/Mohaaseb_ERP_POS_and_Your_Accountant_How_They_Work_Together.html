How the Three Pieces Work Together in Practice

  1. A sale happens at the POS. The cashier completes the transaction — cash, card, or credit.
  2. Inventory updates instantly. The ERP layer deducts the sold items from stock in real time, across every branch.
  3. The accounting entry posts itself. Revenue, cost of goods sold, and tax are recorded automatically — no manual journal entry needed.
  4. Your accountant reviews, not re-enters. Instead of spending days rebuilding the books from receipts, your محاسب reviews accurate, ready-made statements.
