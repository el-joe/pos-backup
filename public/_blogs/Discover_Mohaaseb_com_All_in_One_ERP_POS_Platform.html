What Is Mohaaseb.com?

Mohaaseb.com is the home of Mohaaseb's cloud financial management software — a platform where retailers, restaurants, distributors, and service businesses manage sales, stock, and accounts from one place, without needing separate software for each function.

A Quick Platform Tour

Dashboard

A live overview of sales, expenses, and stock levels the moment you log in — no waiting for end-of-day reports.

POS Module

A fast, simple checkout screen designed for cashiers, connected directly to inventory and accounting in real time.

ERP Module

Full accounting: invoices, expenses, purchase orders, account trees, and multi-branch reporting, all generated automatically from daily operations.

Inventory Module

Barcode scanning, low-stock alerts, and multi-location stock transfers, kept accurate because every POS sale updates it instantly.
