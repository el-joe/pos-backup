Why Purchasing and Inventory Management Are Linked

When purchasing runs separately from inventory tracking, businesses either over-order out of caution or run out of stock at the worst moment. mohaaseb.com ties every supplier invoice to the items it affects, so your stock count and your purchasing history always tell the same story.
