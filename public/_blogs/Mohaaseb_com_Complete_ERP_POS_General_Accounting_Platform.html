One Platform for Three Core Business Needs

Instead of stitching together a POS terminal, a separate inventory spreadsheet, and a third-party accounting tool, mohaaseb.com gives you all three in one login. Every module shares the same data — products, customers, branches, and the general ledger — so nothing gets duplicated or lost in translation between systems.

  • POS for fast, reliable checkout at any branch or register.
  • ERP for purchasing, stock control, and multi-branch operations.
  • General cloud accounting for a live, always-accurate set of books.

How ERP, POS, and General Cloud Accounting Connect Inside Mohaaseb

When a cashier completes a sale on the POS, mohaaseb automatically reduces the item from inventory (ERP) and posts the corresponding journal entry to the general ledger (حسابات عامة سحابي) — all in the same instant. There is no export, no import, and no end-of-day reconciliation step required.

Tip: Because mohaaseb.com keeps ERP, POS, and general accounting on one shared dataset, your monthly close can start the moment the month ends — not days later once everyone's spreadsheets are collected.
