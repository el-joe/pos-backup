The Complete ERP Guide for Mohaaseb.com Users

Whether you're new to ERP or migrating from spreadsheets, this guide walks through how Mohaaseb handles ERP essentials — and how it connects directly to your POS so nothing is entered twice.

What ERP Means Inside Mohaaseb

On mohaaseb.com, ERP isn't a separate module you have to configure — it's the core of the platform. Accounting, purchasing, and inventory are built in from day one and connected to every sale made through the POS.

Core ERP Modules

ModuleWhat it does
General LedgerAutomatically posts every POS sale and purchase as a journal entry.
InventoryTracks stock levels in real time as items are sold or received.
PurchasingManages supplier orders and updates stock and cost data on receipt.
ReportingGenerates profit & loss, balance sheet, and stock reports on demand.

How ERP and POS Work Together in Mohaaseb

Every transaction at the POS terminal flows straight into the ERP: the sale reduces stock, records revenue, and updates the customer's account — all without a separate export or import step. Your محاسب never has to reconcile two sets of numbers.
